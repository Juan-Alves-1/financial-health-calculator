Your Financial Health: Dreamer ☁️

Risk level: High

A dreamer is someone who has the desire to improve financial control, but does not know exactly how much they earn and spend. By not having clear goals due to a lack of planning, you expect external short-term solutions and forget that financial life is the result of personal decisions and long-term attitudes.

You not always save or invest appropriately due to a lack of knowledge of financial product options.
